# NOTICAS
Hi!!!!
I'm Mariana, and noticas has always been an idea i've had in my dreams. A website to help others have a personalized experience learning.
I come from Colombia, where our education is impossed, if you can't keep up with the metjod they use to teach or the type of classes,
then you will have to repeat them until you pass. Noticas is my little piece of imagination of a system that knows that every person
has different necessities at the moment of learning, and adapting to the person's level and skills will ensure a constant progress and a
motivation on keeping up with the marvelous experience of learning.

Learning should be enjoyable and adaptative to each person, and in the moment we understand better that and we work for the goal
of equality of support in learning, we'll move on as a society.
